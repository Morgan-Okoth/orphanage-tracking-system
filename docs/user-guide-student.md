# Student User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Register an Account

1. Go to the registration page and fill in your details:
   - Full name, email address, phone number (+254XXXXXXXXX format)
   - Choose role: **Student**
   - Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
2. Submit the form. Your account will be **pending approval** by an administrator.
3. You will receive an email notification once your account is approved.

### 2. Log In

Once approved, log in with your email and password. Your session lasts 1 hour; you will be prompted to log in again after it expires.

---

## Submitting a Financial Request

1. From your dashboard, click **New Request**.
2. Fill in the request form:
   - **Request Type**: School Fees, Medical Expenses, Supplies, Emergency, or Other
   - **Amount**: Enter the amount in KES (max KES 1,000,000)
   - **Reason**: Describe why you need the funds (minimum 10 characters)
3. **Upload supporting documents** (required — at least one):
   - Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG
   - Maximum file size: 10MB per file
   - Examples: fee structure, medical invoice, receipt
4. Click **Submit Request**.

You will receive an email confirmation and can track the status from your dashboard.

---

## Tracking Your Request

Your request moves through these stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Submitted** | Your request has been received |
| **Under Review** | An administrator is reviewing it |
| **Pending Documents** | Additional documents are required from you |
| **Approved** | Approved by Admin Level 1, awaiting verification |
| **Verified** | Verified by the auditor, payment will be processed |
| **Paid** | Payment has been sent to your M-Pesa number |
| **Rejected** | Request was not approved (reason provided) |

---

## Responding to Document Requests

If your request status changes to **Pending Documents**:

1. Open the request from your dashboard.
2. Read the administrator's note explaining what is needed.
3. Upload the additional documents using the **Upload Documents** button.
4. Your request will automatically return to **Under Review**.

---

## Viewing Comments

Administrators may leave comments on your request. You can view these in the request detail page and reply to provide clarification.

---

## Notifications

You will receive email and SMS notifications for:
- Account approval or rejection
- Request status changes
- New comments on your requests
- Payment confirmation

To view your notification history, click the bell icon in the top navigation bar.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does approval take?**
Account approval typically takes 1–3 business days. Request review takes 3–7 business days.

**Can I edit my request after submitting?**
Yes, but only while the status is **Submitted**. Once an administrator starts reviewing it, you cannot edit it.

**What if my request is rejected?**
You will receive an email with the rejection reason. You may submit a new request with the required corrections.

**How will I receive payment?**
Payment is sent via M-Pesa to the phone number registered on your account.
